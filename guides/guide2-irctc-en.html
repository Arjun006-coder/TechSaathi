How to Book Train Tickets on IRCTC

1. Create IRCTC Account (if not already)

  1. Go to www.irctc.co.in or download IRCTC Rail Connect App.
  2. Click on Register / Sign Up.
  3. Fill in name, mobile number, email, password, and address.
  4. Verify your mobile and email using OTP.

2. Login to IRCTC

  1. Open the website or app.
  2. Enter your Username and Password â†’ Tap Login.

3. Search for Trains

  1. Enter From (source), To (destination), and date of travel.
  2. Tap on Search Trains.
  3. Select a train from the list.
  4. Check availability and fare.

4. Select Class and Book Ticket

  1. Choose class like Sleeper, 3AC, 2AC, etc.
  2. Tap on Book Now.

5. Enter Passenger Details

  1. Enter name, age, gender, and berth preference.
  2. Add more passengers if needed (up to 6).
  3. Enter ID proof number (Aadhaar, PAN, etc.).

6. Make Payment

  1. Select pay now and choose payment method: UPI, Card, Net Banking, Wallet.
  2. Complete the payment.
  3. Ticket will be received on SMS and Email.

7. View or Cancel Tickets

  1. Go to My Bookings section.
  2. View, download, or cancel your ticket.
