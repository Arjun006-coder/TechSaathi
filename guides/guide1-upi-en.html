How to Use UPI Apps (PhonePe Example)

What is UPI?

UPI (Unified Payments Interface) is a real-time payment system that allows you to send and receive money between bank accounts using your mobile phone.

Popular UPI apps include PhonePe, Google Pay, BHIM, and Paytm.

What You Need

  • A smartphone with internet connection
  • A mobile number linked to your bank account
  • Your ATM/debit card for setting UPI PIN

Step-by-Step UPI Setup (PhonePe Example)

  1. Install the PhonePe app from the Play Store or App Store.
  2. Open the app and allow permissions (SMS, Contacts).
  3. Enter your mobile number and verify via OTP.
  4. Search and select your bank from the list.
  5. PhonePe will auto-detect your bank account.
  6. Enter your ATM card details to set UPI PIN.
  7. Create a 4 or 6-digit UPI PIN.
  8. Setup complete! Your UPI is now active.
